Get nudged before your next call. No more "wait, that started two minutes ago" panic.

30 min, 1 hour, or whatever you pick. Pomodoro fans, chaos gremlins — all welcome.

HOW IT WORKS
01
Download & install

Run the installer. ~10 seconds. Windows will be dramatic about it — click "Run anyway".

02
Pick your style

Choose how you want to be interrupted. Set the interval. Try not to pick "Random" and forget about it.

03
Hit start

App goes to tray. You go back to work. You will be reminded. There is no escape.

04
Actually pause

Stand up. Stretch. Walk around. The app did its job. Now do yours.
